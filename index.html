About Me

Why did I leave a cushy banking job to venture into the tech industry? Because being able to work from anywhere at any time is a rare opportunity. For example, following a suggestion from his wife, Instagram co-founder Kevin Systrom first added filters to his product at a bed and breakfast in Mexico. Jordan Wardle built a viral game because he knew his girlfriend enjoyed word games. Those stories will always inspire me! The idea of being able to build something you see a need for - or something just for fun - is incredibly exciting.

I greatly value the freedom that developing web apps provides. Having this knowledge allows me to pursue passions that I otherwise might not be able to. I am thrilled to be on this journey. Reach out if you'd like to collaborate!
