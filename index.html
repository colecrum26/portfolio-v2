About Me

Why did I leave a cushy banking job to venture into the tech industry? Because being able to work on something that interests you from anywhere at any time is a special opportunity. For example, following a suggestion from his wife, Instagram co-founder Kevin Systrom first added filters to his product at a bed and breakfast in Mexico. Or Jordan Wardle, who built a viral game because he knew his girlfriend enjoyed word games.

Stories like those will always inspire me! The idea of being able to build something you see a need for - or something just for fun - is incredibly exciting.

I focus on building simple yet elegant experiences and deeply enjoy the freedom that developing web apps provides. Having this knowledge allows me to pursue other passions of mine, like mountaineering. I am thrilled to be on this journey.
